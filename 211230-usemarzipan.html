On a mission to empower wineries

We are building commerce software for wine makers. Software to make your life easier and allow you to focus on the important things: making fantastic wine and your equally fantastic customers.

By combining the most important features you need into one simple, modern, platform you will be able to sell wherever you need - online, your cellar door, events, wholesale - and market your business effectively whilst gaining the insights you need to manage and grow your business.

Plus, no need to worry about hosting your website, which cart or marketing software to use, or which payment provider. Marzipan takes care of it all.
